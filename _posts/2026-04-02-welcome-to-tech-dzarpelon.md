Hello Tech World!

Welcome to my blog, I'm really glad you're here!

In this first post, I want to welcome you to my little tech corner, where I'll share the techpassions of the time!

As of today, my main focus outside of work is automation and AI, and here I'll share my journey exploring both.

I'll also share ideas and opinions about the future of the tech landscape and how I think it might unfold. Especially in this AI‑heavy era.

The goal here is to communicate in an informal but precise way and share my learning and knowledge journey. Many of the things you'll see here will be experiments, tests, and labs as I explore different parts of the AI and automation ecosystem together with any other tech topics that I'll be passionate/using at some point.

I truly believe technology should empower humans to achieve and build great things. Because of that, technologies that don't contribute to that goal probably won't resonate with me, which means some trending topics in the tech world might not appear here very often.

I hope you enjoy what you find here and that, in some way, it helps you achieve your goals and passions. If those bring you more success in your work or life all the better!
